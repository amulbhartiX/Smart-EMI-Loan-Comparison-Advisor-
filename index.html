💳

Rule of 1/3

In the first third of your loan tenure, most of your EMI goes toward interest. Prepaying in this phase yields maximum savings.

📊

50/30/20 Loan Rule

Ensure your total debt EMIs don't exceed 40% of take-home pay. A healthy EMI-to-income ratio is under 30%.

🏦

Floating vs Fixed

Floating rates track RBI's repo rate and may decrease over time. Fixed rates offer payment certainty but are typically 1-2% higher.
